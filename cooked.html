About Prepared & Cooked Foods

QSFF Agri offers complete solutions for the cooked-food industry, covering marination, frying, steaming, roasting, and braising. Our lines ensure consistent quality, efficient energy use, and food safety compliance.

  • Custom-designed production lines by category
  • Automated temperature & humidity control
  • Integrated sterilization & packaging section
  • Optimized layout for hygiene and workflow

Cooked Food Categories & Equipment

Marinated Products

Vacuum tumblers and marinators ensure even flavor penetration and tender texture.

Fried Products

Continuous fryers for uniform color and crispness with oil filtering systems.

Steamed / Boiled Products

Steam ovens, forming machines, sausage filling and tying systems.

Roasted Products

Braised / Stewed Products

Public Equipment

Sterilization & Packaging

The final section integrates boiling, sterilization, cooling, and vacuum sealing. QSFF Agri’s high-temperature retort and automatic vacuum packaging systems ensure product safety, long shelf life, and freshness.
